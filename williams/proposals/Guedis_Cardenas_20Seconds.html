OUR PROGRAM

Our latest product is a 16+ Week Guide to help you lead a greater serene, joyful, and loving life. It is the research and development of data gathered from encounters and embraces with a full spectrum of magnificent individuals. From Vice Presidents who manage multimillion dollar companies, to warriors who have been victorious with their addictions for more than 15+ years, and to homeless souls who understand the beauty of, It’s a phenomenal day to be alive.

Our program is not only founded on encounters with former President Bill Clinton, Google engineers, Goldman Sachs/J.P. Morgan bankers, and researchers from Stanford, MIT, Yale, and UPenn, but also positive psychology research, interdisciplinary studies, and age old wisdom. At the heart of our program lies a uniquely developed algorithm, thanks to our Computer Science approach to Positive Psychology.

We promote this: just 20 seconds a day of creating new habits of gratitude, conscious acts of kindness, meditation, forgiveness, nutrition, and more, can lead to a healthier, faithful thriving, serene, joyful, and loving life, thereby skyrocketing your performance in relationships, the workplace, academia, and play.

PROGRAM'S GOAL

To help you lead a healthier, thriving, serene, joyful, and loving life. We prepare and empower you to explore and educate yourself in not only the miracles we discovered together, but also others that are important to you. By the end of 16+ weeks, we want to leave you a better person with a mind that is not regretting for lost joys or shame for things badly done or fear and stress for the future – a more mindful, optimistic and hopeful mind. A body that is relaxed, poised, well rested, nutritionally healthy, energized by exercise, and devoid of exhaustion. And a soul that is kinder, faithful, forgiving, gratuitous, playful and more courageous. Because when this happens, you become smarter, stronger, more motivated, and more successful in all that you do – relationships, career, education, and play. We want you, a beautiful living miracle, to overflow with health, faith, prosperity, serenity, joy, and love – a soul that dares to not only read, think, speak, and write, but also share and grow.

PROGRAM'S APPROACH

We developed an algorithm that maximizes the probability of creating and sticking to a habit by dynamically adapting to your current amount of mental, physical, and spiritual energy and time. Fusing our algorithm and program with your neuroplastic brain allows you to lead a heathier, faithful, thriving, serene, joyful, and loving life – its starts with only 20 seconds a day!

ULTIMATE GOAL

The guide (found at 20sec.org) currently takes a minimal potent experiential learning approach. Rather than disclosing the research and why the program is effective, I leave it to reader to proactively discover them. But the guide's peripheral persuasion approach requires a leap of faith. While some may be willing to take it, other will not, therefore, this summer, I will create a product that follows a central route to persuasion. It will be a motivational memoir that not only adapts the guide into a story, but also reveals the algorithm, the why and the research that supports it.
